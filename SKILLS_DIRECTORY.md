## 🎨 Design & UI Development

### [frontend-design](file:///.agents/skills/frontend-design/SKILL.md)
*   **Use for**: Building distinctive, production-grade interfaces from scratch.
*   **When to call**: Creating new pages, landing sections, or unique components that need to look "human-designed" rather than generic AI.

### [impeccable](file:///.agents/skills/impeccable/SKILL.md)
*   **Use for**: Professional design review, polishing, and specialized design commands.
*   **Sub-commands**:
    *   `$impeccable polish`: Final quality pass.
    *   `$impeccable delight`: Add personality/motion.
    *   `$impeccable distill`: Simplify complex UIs.
    *   `$impeccable audit`: Check accessibility/performance.

### [ui-ux-pro-max](file:///.agents/skills/ui-ux-pro-max/SKILL.md)
*   **Use for**: Expert UI/UX patterns and design system intelligence.
*   **When to call**: When you need a specific industry pattern (like a SaaS dashboard) or want to ensure your layout follows established UX laws.

### [ui-animation](file:///C:/Users/peter/.gemini/antigravity/skills/ui-animation/SKILL.md)
*   **Use for**: Advanced motion, gestures, and fluid transitions.
*   **When to call**: When you want "StudyProx" to feel alive with springs, drags, and smooth page reveals.
