# StudyProx: Feature Ecosystem

StudyProx is engineered to bridge the "Intent-Action Gap" by transforming academic scheduling into active, focused study blocks. Each feature is designed to eliminate cognitive friction and enforce deep focus through a premium, OS-integrated aesthetic.

---

## 1. AI Task Architect™
*Solving: "Task Paralysis" & "Planning Fatigue"*

The Architect automates the deconstruction of overwhelming goals into actionable, micro-sprints.
- **Generative Scaffolding**: Uses AI complexity settings (Simple, Standard, Depth) to suggest a logical sequence of steps.
- **One-Click Commitment**: Instantly transforms an abstract goal into a scheduled, focus-ready objective.
- **Adaptive Adjustment**: Coming soon - dynamically suggests re-architecting tasks that are frequently skipped.

## 2. Smart Transition System
*Solving: "Manual Labor" between Planning & Execution*

Our "Friction-to-Focus" engine automatically syncs with academic schedules to reduce the cognitive effort of starting a session.
- **Next Block Detection**: Identifies upcoming seminars or lectures from your integrated schedule.
- **Prepare Focus Button**: A one-tap transition that initializes the focus environment for the upcoming academic block.
- **Contextual Awareness**: Automatically tags sessions with relevant subjects (LAW, STEM, MATH, etc.) for accurate analytics.

## 3. Hard-Lock Focus Timer
*Solving: "Gateway Distractions" & "Audio Termination Issues"*

A high-integrity focus environment that physically and digitally mirrors your study intent.
- **Persistence Shield**: Optimized OS-level integration that prevents audio termination and maintains timer state even across screen locks.
- **App Suppression**: Automatically restricts access to distracting "feed" apps (Instagram, Discord, TikTok) during active sessions.
- **Abort Focus (Integrity Loss)**: A high-friction exit path that emphasizes the psychological cost of breaking a focus streak.

## 4. Spaced Repetition Library
*Solving: "Rigid Structures" & "Lack of Adaptive Retention"*

An integrated filing system for knowledge retention that adapts to your learning pace.
- **Dynamic Due Dates**: Automatically flags card decks as "Due Today" based on your past accuracy and retention intervals.
- **Subject Categorization**: Knowledge is filed by academic subject, ensuring a direct link between study time and knowledge mastery.
- **Recall Visualization**: High-accuracy badges and review indicators turn isolation into measurable mastery.

## 5. Peer Intelligence & Social Analytics
*Solving: "Isolation" & "Lack of Social Proof"*

Transform academic consistency into a shared habit through high-fidelity data visualization.
- **Friends Leaderboard**: A real-time, hour-based ranking that sorts by most active study effort.
- **Weekly Consistency Peak**: Visualizes your "Peak State" to identify when you are most cognitively productive.
- **Subject Efficiency Heatmaps**: Maps your effort across subjects to ensure a balanced academic commitment.

- **Architecture**: React + Tailwind CSS
- **Design Language**: Academic-Noir / Scholarly-Minimalism
- **Device Emulation**: Integrated iPhone Frame for high-fidelity mobile prototyping.
